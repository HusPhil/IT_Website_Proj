LIFE--LIFE--LIFE--LIFE

The Meaning Of Life

The sun knew when to rise The flowers knew when to bloom And so, I envy the way of knowing As I wonder the meaning OF LIFE

As a student I was enchanted to believe that life holds meaning. But as I continue to this path, the more I struggle to keep on believing. The stress, workload, and pressure keeps on growing. Often still awake until the deep dusk, where frustration is the only dawning.

Often I question myself, does life truly have meaning? Does my effort, achievements, and suffering really matter? Dark thoughts, sad thoughts, kept creeping in on my skin. And I can't help but feel anxious as they swallow me from within.

Still, I believe that from the bitterness of life comes its meaning. That sadness cannot exist without joy. That crying comes before laughing. And with a context of a colorless life. Exist one that is full of colors.

There exist a light of hope that guides on my destination. We share the bitterness of achievements and the bitterness of failure.

Life can be black and white but its meaning is somewhere in between, and with cruelty of fate, I will keep on dancing. for them, Because of them, even if meaningless, life is still worth living.

LIFE--LIFE--LIFE--LIFE
